Welcome to Humanistic Da Vinci

Our association promotes a civil society, dedicated to the moral and intellectual improvement of people. Our Institution is based on the principles of Liberty, Equality, and Fraternity, and our motto is Science, Justice, Work.

We are present in all areas: culture, science, contemporary challenges, and the well-being of our society. We aim to contribute to the development of a more equitable, fairer, and more inclusive society.

We work for the utopia of universal brotherhood. That utopia is our horizon, and we trust that one day it will be our reality.
