About us

We are the Leonardo Da Vinci Association, an Association founded in 2020 in Italy, with Codice Fiscale nº 91599030151. Our association promotes a civil society, dedicated to the moral and intellectual improvement of people. Our Institution is based on the principles of Liberty, Equality and Fraternity, and our motto is Science, Justice, Work.”

We are present in all areas: culture, science, contemporary challenges, the well-being of our society. We try to contribute our vision to contribute to the development of a more equitable, fairer and more inclusive society.

We work for the utopia of universal brotherhood. That utopia is our horizon and we trust that one day it will be our reality.
